# Sample Questions for Testing

Upload any text-based PDF and test the RAG chatbot using these questions.

## General Questions

1. What is this document about?
2. Summarize the main points of the document.
3. What are the most important details in this PDF?
4. What problem does the document discuss?
5. What conclusion is given in the document?

## Specific Questions

6. What are the key requirements mentioned?
7. What tools or technologies are discussed?
8. Are there any deadlines or dates mentioned?
9. Who is the target audience of this document?
10. What steps should be followed according to the document?

## Out-of-Document Test

Ask a question that is not answered in the PDF.  
The chatbot should respond that it could not find the information in the uploaded document.
